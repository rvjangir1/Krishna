This is Krishna's website. Here you will know some information about Krishna

Krishna is supreme personality of Godhead.

Krishna is very kind, merciful and all attractive. He is unlimited, and full of Sac-chid-ananda.

Fours ways to remember Krishna:

  • Name
  • Form (Roop)
  • Qualities (Guna)
  • Pastimes (Leela)
Hare Kṛṣṇa Hare Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa Kṛṣṇa Hare Hare Hare Rāma Hare Rāma Rāma Rāma Hare Hare
Krishna has unlimited Forms. He appeared again and again, millennium after millennium to re-establish the dharma and to enjoy with his pure devotees.
